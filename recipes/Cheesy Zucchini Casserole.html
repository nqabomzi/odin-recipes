Cheesy Zucchini Casserole

Description

This zucchini casserole with bread cubes, onion, and Cheddar is very easy to prepare for a deliciously cheesy vegetable side dish.

Ingredients

    4 slices bread, cubed Â¼ cup melted butter 2 cups cubed zucchini 1 large onion, finely chopped 1 large egg, beaten 1 teaspoon garlic salt 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

  • 1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • 2. Place bread cubes in a medium bowl and pour melted butter over top. Add zucchini, onion, egg, and garlic salt; mix well.
  • 3. Transfer the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish and top with cheese
  • 4. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Uncover the dish and bake for another 30 minutes.
